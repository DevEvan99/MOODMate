Literature Survey

Figure 1. Collective Break-up Experience from 107 People - Rebel Love

In everyday actions, humans display a range of emotions, and these emotions have a great impact on human lives and relationships. Depression affects more than 280 million people worldwide [1]. Women are 50% more likely to suffer from depression than men. More than 700,000 people die by suicide every year. In the rapidly changing digital world, mobile applications offer unprecedented opportunities to integrate modern technologies for emotional well-being, changes in relationships, and many other complex human needs. In this context, research focuses on creating a mobile application using modern technological solutions to reduce people's stress and improve human emotional well-being, as well as building and strengthening meaningful relationships. Emotion recognition and sentiment analysis have appeared as powerful tools to understand and respond to users' emotional states in real time. In recent years, convolutional neural network (CNN) has often been used for sentiment analysis [2]. Stress can be assessed in a variety of ways, including mental, physiological, emotional, and physical activities [3].

However, through the proposed system, the attention has been focused on the development of human relations beyond the development of social skills. It can be shown that a virtual therapist with special knowledge of human relations has been created in the proposed system to provide that awareness. Overall, through the proposed system, the focus is not only on developing social skills and social cognition but also on building human relationships, not limited to one mental problem. This provides a suitable environment for the proper use of social skills.

Research Problem & Solution

Proposed Problem

How to identify human emotion and do sentiment analysis for Relationship Improvement?

The research paper primarily investigates how the relationship between technology and mental health can positively impact the human. Specifically, it aims to explore how advanced AI algorithms, sentiment analysis, and VR technology can be employed to help individuals understand and manage their emotions better while also developing and strengthening meaningful interpersonal relationships.


Proposed Solution

The proposed solution in the research paper involves the development of a mobile application that integrates various modern technological solutions to enhance users' emotional well-being and relationships. Key components of the solution include

01. Emotion Recognition and Sentiment Analysis: The application uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to analyze users' facial expressions and audio inputs to detect emotions in real-time. This allows for a deeper understanding of users' emotional states.

02. Personalized Recommendations: Based on the sentiment analysis, the application provides tailored recommendations to users. These recommendations can include mini-games for relaxation, and in severe cases of emotional distress, contact information for professional counseling services.

03. AI-Powered VR Character: The application features a virtual reality character powered by AI, designed to act as a virtual companion. This VR character offers practical advice and support, helping users to strengthen their emotional connections and communication skills, particularly useful for those starting new relationships.

04. Stress Reduction and Emotional Support: The overall goal of the application is to reduce stress and improve emotional well-being by leveraging advanced AI and VR technologies. The solution aims to provide a comprehensive platform that supports users in managing stress and enhancing their interpersonal relationships.

Research Objectives

Emotion Recognition Using Audio and Video:

This objective focuses on developing models to identify human emotions through audio and video data. The project involves training convolutional neural network (CNN) models on audio and visual data to detect various emotions such as anger, happiness, and sadness. Techniques like spectrograms for audio data and ResNet50 for video data are uilized to improve accuracy in emotion detection.

Sentiment Analysis:

The goal here is to analyze textual data to determine the sentiment of the user. This involves using the DASS-21 questionnaire to assess levels of depression, anxiety, and stress. A Random Forest classifier model is employed to process and interpret the sentiment from the text data, offering insights into the emotional state of the user.

Development of a Virtual Reality (VR) Environment:

Creating an immersive VR environment where users can interact with virtual characters to express their emotions and practice social skills. The VR setup is designed using Unity, with integration of OpenAI and AWS services for realistic interactions. The VR environment aims to provide a supportive space for users to improve their emotional well-being and build meaningful relationships.

Integration of AI for Personalized User Experience:

This objective involves the integration of AI technologies to offer personalized recommendations and support to users based on their emotional state. By analyzing the data collected through audio, video, and text, the system provides customized feedback and interventions to help users manage their emotions and improve their relationships.

Methodology

Figure 2. High Level Architecture of the system.

The proposed system have 4 main components. They are;

  1. AI Enhance VR environment for Mental Health & Relationship
  2. Recognize an emotion using image processing
  3. Recognize an emotion using Audio processing
  4. Sentiment analysis Implementation

The proposed solution aims to provide an intelligent approach for stakeholders, and researchers to identify depression that can affect humans and improve human emotional well-being as well as build and strengthen meaningful relationships. The registered users of the system can upload the video call feed. The video is sent to the Amazon Web Services (AWS) back-end server. These videos are processed in the server by the designed CNN models for emotion identification. CNN architecture is defined using the Keras API from TensorFlow for AI-enhanced audio-based sentiment recognition. The RestNet50 CNN architecture has been defined to recognize an emotion using image processing. Emotional severity is determined using the Random Forest Classifier model. Then it shows the remedies that can be given to the user. Ultimately designing a VR character to improve relationships allows users to express their thoughts, and practice social skills.
